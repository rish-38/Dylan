LOGO CONCEPT

Dark Slate Blue #264257
Blue tones are linked to the sea and sky, offering a sense of tranquility and peace. It signifies versatility and classic appeal.
"Dylan" means "forever flowing" and "born from the sea," reflecting timeless style. The logo, in the Maberk font, replaces the "A" with a wave symbol, tying to its sea-inspired essence. The tagline "Ride the waves" encourages embracing trends and staying effortlessly fashionable.

SLOGAN CONCEPT

The tagline "Ride the waves" reinforces Dylan's identity, encouraging individuals to embrace trends, stay fashionable, and flow effortlessly with the ever-changing currents of style. Inspired by iconic taglines like Apple's "Think Different" and Nike's "Just Do It", it reflects a bold, modern spirit while staying true to the brand's sea-inspired essence.
