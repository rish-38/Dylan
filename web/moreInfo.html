ABOUT THE BRAND

Dylan is a fashion brand inspired by the sea, embodying timeless style and elegance. The name Dylan, meaning "forever flowing" and "born from the sea," reflects its connection to the ever-changing yet enduring nature of the ocean. The logo is designed using the Maberk font, with a creative twist where the letter "A" is replaced by a wave-shaped symbol, tying the brand closely to its ocean-inspired essence. The tagline, "Ride the Waves," encourages individuals to embrace trends effortlessly, staying fashionable with confidence and grace. The brands color palette enhances its identity, featuring Dark Slate Blue (#264257) to signify the tranquility and versatility of the sea and sky, Light Blue (#C5DEE2) to evoke serenity, Bright Orange (#FD6941) for a burst of energy and vibrancy, and White (#FFFFFF) for timeless simplicity. Together, these elements create a visual and conceptual harmony, positioning Dylan as a brand that blends modern trends with classic appeal, making it a strong competitor to fashion giants like Zara.
