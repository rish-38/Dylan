LED WALL AD MOCKUP
RADIO AD

This radio ad uses humor and relatable scenarios to introduce Dylan as a new fashion brand targeting Gen Z. Here's how it works: Relatable Setup:

The ad begins with a funny situation where multiple young people mistakenly call an "uncle," thinking he is Dylan. This creates curiosity about who or what "Dylan" is. Humor and Frustration: The uncle's growing confusion and frustration add comedic value, keeping listeners engaged and entertained. Clear Brand Introduction: After the humor, the ad smoothly transitions to a narrator explaining that Dylan is a trendy fashion brand that makes you effortlessly fashionable. This approach ensures the ad is memorable, creates curiosity, and effectively communicates Dylan's essence as a stylish, youth-oriented brand

VIDEO AD

The DYLAN video ad, "Ride the Waves of Fashion," tells a creative, emotional, and stylish story. It begins in a therapy room, where a patient with memory loss reveals they're searching for something "new, fashionable." A dynamic montage shows their search through social media and fashion inspiration. The doctor's quirky guitar strumming adds humor and intrigue. The story shifts into the patient's memory, where they follow a mysterious note and map, eventually discovering a DYLAN shirt. Back in the therapy room.
